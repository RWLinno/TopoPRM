\section{Experiments}
\label{sec:experiments}

\paragraph{Setup.}
We use Qwen3-14B~\citep{qwen3} as the base model for all experiments.
SFT and GRPO training are conducted using MS-Swift~\citep{msswift} on
a curated dataset of mathematical critique examples. We evaluate on four
benchmarks: MATH500, GSM8K~\citep{cobbe2021gsm8k}, CMATH, and GaoKao.
Critique accuracy measures whether the model correctly identifies the
first error step (or correctly confirms a valid solution). Average
response length (Avg-Len) is measured in tokens. Additional training
details and hyperparameters are provided in Appendix~\ref{app:details}.
